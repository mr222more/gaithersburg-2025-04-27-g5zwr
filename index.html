About CustomEuroGlass

Founded in 2010, CustomEuroGlass began with a vision to bring European-inspired shower glass designs to Gaithersburg homes. Our founder's 20+ years of experience in custom glass installation and commitment to quality craftsmanship has established us as the region's premier shower glass specialist.

Our mission is to transform ordinary bathrooms into extraordinary spaces through innovative design, superior materials, and exceptional service. We believe in transparency, integrity, and delivering value that exceeds expectations. Every installation is backed by our lifetime warranty and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our shower glass installations.

How long does installation typically take?

Most standard installations are completed within one day. Custom projects may require 1-2 days depending on complexity and additional features.

What type of warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive lifetime warranty on both materials and installation, covering any manufacturer defects or installation-related issues.

What glass options are available?

We offer 3/8" and 1/2" tempered safety glass in clear, frosted, and patterned options. All glass meets or exceeds industry safety standards.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financing providers to offer flexible payment plans with competitive rates. Ask about our current promotions.

What maintenance is required?

Our glass features a protective coating that minimizes water spots. Simple weekly cleaning with a squeegee and mild cleaner is all that's needed.
